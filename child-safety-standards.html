Child Safety Standards for TIPMatch

Last updated: July 22, 2026

TIPMatch ("we", "our", "the App") is committed to protecting children from sexual exploitation and abuse. This page outlines our published standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), in line with Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

TIPMatch has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any form of child sexual exploitation. TIPMatch is intended solely for users 18 years of age and older, and we do not knowingly allow minors to create or use accounts.

2. Prevention Measures

  • Mandatory student ID verification to confirm eligible users are enrolled students of legal age
  • Age declaration and target audience restriction (18+) during account creation
  • Ongoing content moderation of profiles, posts, and messages
  • Proactive removal of accounts or content suspected of violating these standards

3. In-App Reporting

TIPMatch allows users to report child safety concerns directly within the app. Reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team, and appropriate action is taken, including account suspension or termination and reporting to relevant authorities where required.

4. Reporting to Authorities

We comply with all relevant child safety laws and cooperate with regional and national authorities. Suspected CSAM or CSAE will be reported to the appropriate law enforcement or child protection agencies, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.

5. Designated Point of Contact

Our designated point of contact for child safety matters is ready to address questions regarding our CSAE prevention practices and compliance.

6. Policy Updates

These standards may be updated periodically to reflect evolving best practices and legal requirements. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated standards.
