Privacy Policy for TIPMatch

Last updated: July 22, 2026

TIPMatch ("we", "our", "the App") is a dating/social app for T.I.P. students. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and login credentials
  • Verification data: student ID photos, used only to confirm eligibility
  • Profile content: photos, bio, preferences you choose to share
  • User-generated content: posts, messages, and interactions with other users
  • Device information: device type, OS version, and app usage data for troubleshooting

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To verify student status and prevent fraud
  • To match you with other users and enable app features
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To communicate important updates about the app

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • Law enforcement, if required by law

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Student ID images are used solely for verification and are not publicly displayed.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

TIPMatch is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
